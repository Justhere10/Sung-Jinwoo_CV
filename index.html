Sung Jinwoo

The most powerful Hunter

Sung Jinwoo (성진우) is the main protagonist of Solo Leveling. Originally an infamously weak E-Rank Hunter, he got the chance of a lifetime when he was selected as the Player of the System, giving him the unique ability to grow in strength without restraint. As a result, by taking advantage of the opportunities offered to him by the System, Jinwoo eventually rose to power as humanity's greatest hunter and the second Shadow Monarch.

Abilities

  • Immense Strength: Jinwoo possesses incomparably immense levels of physical strength. As a testament to this, he was able to quash the Statue of God, beat Thomas Andre to the brink of death while still holding back, kill the Beast Monarch in four hits, and slice Antares almost completely in half during their fight.
  • Ruler's Authority: Jinwoo is able to move and control objects via telekinesis.
  • Shadow Extraction: Jinwoo is able to extract shadows from the corpses of his enemies and add them to his army.
  • Quicksilver: Jinwoo is able to increase his speed by 30% for a short burst.
  • Stealth: Jinwoo is able to camouflage with his surroundings and hide all traces of presence, essentially turning himself both physically and magically invisible.
